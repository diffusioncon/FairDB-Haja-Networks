FairDB

"A non-profit organisation that starts here to change the way we pay for services."
1. Register for a database.
2. Pay the fee.
3. Money goes to selected charities.

The premise.

As developers we use databases all the time, from MySQL to GraphQL they are critical to almost every system. We want to make a revolutionary change in the way developers think about where their money goes.

The concept.

Databases are usually provided by datacenters, where they fee money spent goes into the company that hosts them. Our idea is to provide a simple Key-Value database storage where the fee money goes to charity.

The impact.

  • Charitable.
  • Eithical.
  • Future innovation.
  • Global impact.
